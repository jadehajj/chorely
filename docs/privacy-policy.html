Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

Chorely ("we", "our", or "us") is a family chore and pocket money tracking app. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our service. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By using Chorely, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information: Email address and display name used to create a parent account via Email/Password, Apple Sign‑In, or Google Sign‑In.
  • Family data: Names and optional avatar images you create for child profiles within your family group.
  • App data: Chore definitions, completion records, pocket money balances, and reward history that you enter into the app.
  • Purchase receipts: Transaction records from Apple's StoreKit when you purchase a subscription tier (processed by Apple; we receive only a receipt confirmation).

We do not collect location data, device contacts, microphone or camera input, advertising identifiers, or any data from children without verifiable parental consent — parent accounts control all data creation.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and sync data across your devices.
  • To display chore assignments, completion history, and balances within your family group.
  • To verify your in-app purchase tier and unlock the appropriate features.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), hosted in Google's secure cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Firebase security rules ensure that each family can only access their own data.

Profile images are stored in Firebase Storage with access restricted to authenticated members of your family group.

Third‑Party Services

Chorely uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Apple Sign‑In & StoreKit — optional social sign-in and in-app purchases.
  • Google Sign‑In — optional social sign-in.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Chorely is designed for families. Child profiles within the app are created and managed exclusively by the parent account holder. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Child profiles contain only a first name and optional avatar image entered by a parent.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child without parental consent, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will delete it.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at jadehajj@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

In-app purchase records may be retained by Apple independently of your Chorely account, in accordance with Apple's own privacy policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jadehajj@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy
